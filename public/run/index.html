🗡️ iSword RUN — the endless slicing runner

The main game: you race forward forever, blobs fly at you, and you cut them for score and money. Money persists between runs and buys upgrades. Four worlds below share that loop but each has its own twist — swipe is the stand-in for your phone-sword swing.

A · Neon Runway THE BASELINE ⚔️ PHONE SWORD

Synthwave highway. Slice gel blobs for coins, cut the red gates before they slam into you, never touch the violet mines. Shop: extra heart, wide blade, golden edge. Supports the real IMU sword: tap 🗡️ Phone Sword in-game to pair your phone (needs the Node relay — npm start, not GitHub Pages).

B · Torii Inkway BRUSH = BLADE + BRIDGE

Ink-painted world. Your stroke cuts demons and becomes a bridge your fox spirit runs on — draw paths to high seals and over thorns. One gesture, two meanings. Limited ink, refilled by cutting.

C · Goo Cavern LIVING ECOSYSTEM

Dive an endless bioluminescent gullet. Blobs split when cut — babies pay 🧬. Ignore blobs and they merge into 2-hit MEGAs. Get splatted and goo covers your screen until you wipe it off.

D · Sky Serpent RHYTHM + RISK

Ride a serpent through a dawn sky that banks into turns. Crystals ride a pounding beat — cut on the pulse for ×2. Grazing near-misses charges DASH: invincible, triple score, sky on fire.
Every world has the full loop: distance score · money that persists · upgrade shop · hearts · death & restart.
